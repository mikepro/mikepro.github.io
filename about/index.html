You have reached the personal site of github user mikepro otherwise known as Michael. A web developer and user of emacs.

One of my main aims with this website is to introuduce emacs in a friendly and approcable way. Emacs has a fearsome reputation of having a step learning curve and being difficult to use. I can still remember the first time I opened up emacs (way back in 2001) and how overwhelemed I felt taking my first frustating steps, getting confused, getting anoyed with cryptic keyboard shortcuts and not understanding what I was doing. This ultimately led to me quiting emacs then and several times over the years. Now 21 years later its an absolute joy to use and I would not be without it.

Whilst I can certainly sympathize with the view that emacs is difficult to learn, knowing what I know now I don’t belive that it has to be that way. Over the comming weeks and months I will write the articles that I wish I had, had access to when I started which I hope can make your journey discovering emacs much smother and more enjoyable.
